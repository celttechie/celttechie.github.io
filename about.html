About

Date Tue 27 May 2014 Tags

Overview

I've always enjoyed fixing things, troubleshooting, solving problems and designing solutions. I approach situations in a logical manner. I ask lots of questions and look at situations from as many angles as I can. I like to research and fully understand the problem and as many variables as I can to come up with a superior solution. I am very detail oriented. I enjoy challenging projects that require me to learn something new along the way. I make a point to deep dive on each new technology I learn so I can properly troubleshoot when things go wrong. I make it a point to know where to get the information I need and to know how to apply it. I don't try to memorize a fix for each problem.

Problem Solving/Troubleshooting

When I was in High School I wrote down the things I liked to do and what I was good at, and a career in engineering seemed to be a good fit. I got my first taste of solving real-world problems when going through the introductory classes in engineering at Colorado School of Mines.
